The Challenge

The Dead End Experience- the ‘Checkout Success’ Page

Every shopping experience at eBay is unique, however everyone who checks out experiences the same dead end - the ‘Checkout Success’ page. Though it’s great that shoppers have completed their shopping goal and made it through checkout, should their experience really be over? What kind of experiences should eBay build to re-engage our user base back into our ecosystem? How can we reinvent the Checkout Success page?

Design Process Overview

Research Goes with Sketches

Target Audience

The Reveler: "Let me discover what I’ll enjoy most"

Exploration

Revelers often go to a new neighborhood and explore the little shops and boutiques. It’s fun, relaxing and they always learn something.

Identity

Shopping is an extension of The Reveler’s personality. They seek for unique and surprising items and follow their own way.

Achievement

At the end of a successful shopping excursion reveler feel justiﬁed and happy. They’re excited to go home and create new combinations and possibilities with other items they own.

Our Concept

Think about the Overall Shopping Experience

Instead of just focusing on the checkout successful page, we analyzed the whole online shopping experience to understand the user journey. We break it down to four stage and came up with solutions that help buyers re-engage into ebay.

Our Solution

The Reveler's New Experience

Revelers Are Wondering Which To Buy Among Several Products They Enjoy
Recommendations From Salesclerk Often Provide Valuable Insights to The Revelers.
After Checkout, Revelers Will Get Excited To Create Their Own World With Their Full Bags

HI-FIDELITY PROTOTYPES

Multitask Exploration

Users can open several tabs with deep press on their loved items which will be stored in the left bar. When they decide to buy one of them, this product will be closed automatically while other items are still waiting for more exploration.

Personalized Recommendation through Interests Group

Users can open several tabs with deep press on their loved items which will be stored in the left bar. When they decide to buy one of them, this product will be closed automatically while other items are still waiting for more exploration.

Create Your Long-term Porfolio

When complete their purchase, users can enter personal collection within profile page. Then they design and create own portfolio, share them in a group to reward themselves.

Future Strategy

Exploration Out Of The Screen

For future improvement of exploration and creation, the recommendation group is a potential concept to develop. There are possibilities for users to explore deals, share purchase stories and ask tips for each other. As more content being generated in the group, users have more opportunities to explore the eBay world. In return, eBay can also increase the viscosity of users. What's more, concept of exploring the real world has been introduced in which users can shoot and tag what they love during travel or other offline exploration. Ebay matches them with the search result and gives the respective recommendation

Design Process

We Start From Asking Questions

We start form asking questions and exploring potential aspects. Then we organized them into categories.

Social

Can it be real time? Who is purchasing at that time? How can I discuss and share my ideas with others? Do they want to show off?

Collection Purchase

What’s people’s habit to buy products? Would they want to buy a single item or a collection? Which group of products can be a collection?

Recommendation

How to really give effective recommendation? What if we can cooperate with some other platforms like medium or classified. Can we provide an interest circle for recommendation?

Real Purchase Experience

What’s the general purchase experience in real world? Can we promote some other products during the waiting experience? Can our process be not linear? What’s the key stakeholders during real purchase experience?

Online Gaming

How to engage users like online game? How would they get enough rewarding? Can they develop or accomplish something during the purchase?

Contextual

Can we bring some meaning to the purchase? How can we contain some memories within the process? How can we tell a story to the users?

Competitor Analysis

We collected several related mobile applications and analyzed their functions and features. We compared different apps experience with ebays to find eBay's identity.
