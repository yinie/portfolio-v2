The Challenge

How can an intelligent digital assistant support the needs of students in 2020?

Siri, GoogleNow, Facebook M, Alexa, Cortana, Luka and myriad other speech or text activated assistants have all started the conversation about what intelligence is and does in system design. What can intelligent digital assistance do to support student in the future?

User Research

What are the issues women in STEM facing?

To gain a better understanding of what are the issues women in STEM are facing, we conducted contextual inquiry and serveral interviews with girls who left STEM after graduation, who stay in STEM and who is pursuing STEM degree in college.

We used affinity diagramming to synthesize our research finding and to find potential “bright spot” that we could leverage on.

Research Insight

Fullfillment

Motivation and interest in STEM mainly comes from achievement of solving problems within the student’s own capability.

Acceptance

Feeling accepted by other people in STEM is critical for women is STEM to engage in the learning process. It encourages them to speak in class and take responsibility in group work.

Confidence

The self-perception as a woman in STEM varies among individuals. Students who stay in STEM tend to have the long-term plan of a career in STEM
