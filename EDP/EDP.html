The Challenge
Encourage People to Use Password Generator

The problem presented to us was that users of 1Password do not use the password generator feature in order to strengthen passwords on their existing accounts or when creating new accounts. Learning how people feel about 1password and their habit of online password is the key to solve this problem.

User Research
Users Attitude to Password Management Software

Nancy Rivers

"Even though I sometimes forget my passowrd for some accounts, I still don't feel it's safe to store your password in another company's software."

William Grant

"I've tried password managment program but they are too complex, so I end up using almost one password. And now I feel it will take a long time to set it up since people all have multiple devices"

Our Solution
Instill Trust to 1Password Redesign

Minimize the Steps

The current password generator is hidden behind menus within the desktop software and it is not an esstential part of the interface. We only kept the integrated browser extension and minimize the interaction to use passwork generator.

Emphasis the Password's Security

By comparing the strenghness of their passwords and the generated passwords, we encourage users to use password generator.

Integrate User's Context

We integrate 1password within the sign up form so users don't need to set up all the account in the software. The browser extension will indicate the strenghness of password according to the website they are visiting.

HI-FIDELITY PROTOTYPES
Provide the Strong Password Whenever You Need

Compare the Passwords' Strength

When users click inside the password field, a suggested password is generated by 1password and presented with the strength. They can click and use the genetated password or keep typing their own. Then the strength of the password appears compared with the strength of the suggested password.

REFLECTION
Embrace You Constraint

Design is not just building a fresh new system. It can also be focusing on a specific detail and dig into it. Focusing on encourage users to use 1password generator, we iterate different ways to compare the strength of the passwords and how to instill trust to our users. I was also impressed by working with people from different background. After two weeks collaboration, we have defintely instill trust to our teammates.
