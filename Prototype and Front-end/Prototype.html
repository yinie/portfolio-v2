Take a Step Back

"Take a Step Back" is a tool for creative workers, especially designers. It takes the advantages of digital product - recording, storing and display, then combine them into traditional tools – the whiteboard and the marker. This tool uses the metaphor “take a step back” to let the user really view the project from a bigger view when the user takes steps back. View the demo video for the design details
Tools: Ardruino, Node.js
